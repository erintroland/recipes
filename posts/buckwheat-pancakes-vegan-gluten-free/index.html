Buckwheat Pancakes (Vegan, Gluten Free)

November 28, 2025

Very good!!

  1. I made the batter the night before and let mine sit in the fridge over night and added a bit almond milk in the morning. That way it was easy to get to pancakes in the morning without having to make the batter.
  2. It made around 6 medium sized pancakes.
  3. Maybe add a little oil to the batter next time to make them a bit more moist.
  4. Too much cinnamon--also maybe experiment with other spices like cardamom.
  5. Raspberries, simple syrup, and rose water would be a great topping.
  6. For a full breakfast, I ate three with some cashew butter and a banana.
