Minestrone Soup (Template)

August 7, 2023

This is pretty close to a specific version of the Kitchen Counter Cooking School soup template. Was nice to see it as an option.

  1. Use what you know about aromatics to add them in the way it makes sense. Also, caramelize the tomato paste a bit with everything to add more flavor.
  2. Use your judgment for when to add veggies. The recipe instructions were a little weird. I didn't add certain veggies to the pot until I added the pasta (e.g. green beans) so they wouldn't be soggy, and switched up the ordering on adding in some of the aromatics.
  3. Making a pot of beans from scratch can be nice (see below), but this turned out just fine with better than bullion.

I also made a pot of beans from scratch, which gave me instant broth and much better beans for the soup. It's pretty easy if you set them out to soak on Sat night and then cook them Sun when you get up. I threw in a quartered onion and some smashed garlic cloves. This link is a nice guide to cooking beans with suggestions for what else to do with them.
