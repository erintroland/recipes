Oatmeal Scotchies

January 15, 2024

Yum! Love these cookies. A tad on the sweet side, but adding salt definitely helps balance.

  1. Cut back a bit on the butterscotch chips--maybe try 3/4 cup.
  2. I forgot the salt in the dough the first time, don't forget this! And the flaky sea salt at the end is a must.
  3. I only needed to bake these for about 8-9 mins. So definitely check at 8 mins.
  4. I followed the tips about the butter in the recipe--don't let it get too soft. My cookies spread nicely but not too much.
