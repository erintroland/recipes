Toasts with Roasted Squash, Maple Syrup Caramelized Onions and Ricotta

November 16, 2014

Wow wow this was amazing. So delicious. I actually just put it over some grains (farro)  instead of toasts, and it was still amazing. I think it's pretty versatile in that way. It takes around 1.5h including cleanup.

Notes

1. If using acorn squash, cook for 25-30 minutes. The skin on mine was very hard in some places, and it's edible it if just cook it for a little longer.

2. Double or triple the onions, you won't be disappointed. And low and slow is the game (don't do medium-high like the recipe says!). Start these first before the squash.

3. Butternut squash cooked all the way through in 15 mins. I don't really think there's a need to flip it over.

4. Also good with some roasted chickpeas you just toss into the oven while roasting the squash. They'll take a bit longer than the squash.
