Mushroom and Potato Soup

February 24, 2020

This was really delicious, I just over salted it. So be careful on the salt next time!

Onions, garlic, herbs, red pepper flakes (or other aromatics)

Mushrooms, red potatoes

Broth (turkey, chickpea, etc)

Farro, white beans

1. Chop garlic and onions.

Saute garlic and onions with a large pinch of salt (can add hearty herbs like thyme and red pepper flakes if desired) until translucent. During, pour in wine when the onions start to stick, and can be generous with the wine.

2. While aromatics are cooking, chop mushrooms and potatoes. Add mushrooms to pot first, letting them saute a bit. Then add potatoes and saute briefly.

3. Add in broth, and some salt. Be careful not to over salt.

4. Simmer for 30 mins or so, then add in farro and white beans, and simmer for another 15 mins or so.

5. Finish with lemon and cream and salt if needed.
