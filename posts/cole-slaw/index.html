Cole Slaw

March 27, 2018

Yum! This was so good:) I didn't even use celery seed or blue cheese, though I'm sure both would be delicious. I also subbed in half of the mayo for 2% fat Greek yogurt, and the dressing was still super full of flavor.

Make sure to chop the cabbage a bit smaller next time--thinly slice the wedge, then chop up again so you don't end up with long thing strands of cabbage.
