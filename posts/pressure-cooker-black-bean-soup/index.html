Pressure Cooker Black Bean Soup

November 15, 2017

This was tasty and I'd make it again. 1. Add more chipotle! I was pretty conservative (a generously rounded teaspoon) and I could not taste it at all. 2. I added in some bell peppers to up the veggies. 3. It does take some time, probably 35-40 mins total in the pressure cooker since it takes awhile to bring it to pressure.
