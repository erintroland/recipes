Sour Cream Biscuits

March 3, 2020

Yum! These are great and easy to make with a soup, and I can find sour cream with no fillers (unlike heavy cream).

Just be careful not to add too much water--mine turned out a bit wet, then were hard to bake and got a bit dried out on the outside compared to the inside,
