Hot Honey Shrimp With Summer Slaw

September 15, 2023

Wow! Some of the best shrimp I've had. Some notes for next time:

  1. Frozen shrimp: put in fridge the night before. Before cooking, take them out and see if there's a frozen chunk in the middle. If so, take all shrimp out of the bag and thaw in warm water for a few mins. Then place on paper towels, pat dry, and let them dry out until ready to bake.
  2. Cooking the shrimp: I roasted them for 3 mins, turned them over, and roasted for another 3 mins. They were opaque and perfectly cooked.
  3. Added some grains (farro) to make this a complete meal.
  4. Because frozen shrimp has a lot of water ,even if you dry it well, add the glaze at the end. So make the hot honey sauce *with* the garlic, then add to shrimp after they are cooked. Also don't forget to add the vinegar at the end, might make the glaze easier to pour.
  5. You can omit the scallions and carrots if you're feeling lazy.
