Whole Roasted Chicken

June 10, 2018

What a simple, cheap, abundant way to make a dinner. And lunches. And more dinners. Page 108 Kitchen Counter Cooking School.

A 6lb chicken costs less than $5 at Aldi. It makes 5 meals of chicken and veggies and at least 5 more of enchiladas or soup or pot pie. Plus stock.

1. More veggies. I added half a cauliflower and 3-4 large carrots and it was maybe enough for 3 meals. They cook down so much when they're in the oven, so just put more next time.

2. Adding in the butter/herbs/salt/lemon under the chicken skin didn't add much to the flavor. Maybe add more next time?

3. It took a it a bit over an hour and a half, and I cooked it a hair too long. It was 160 degrees and 10 minutes later it was 190, so check after 5 mins or so if it's at 160.
