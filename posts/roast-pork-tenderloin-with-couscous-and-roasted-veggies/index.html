Roast Pork Tenderloin with Couscous and Roasted Veggies

July 24, 2023

This was so good! I modified it quite a bit, but I kept the pork the same.

  1. If thermometer is reading ~140, pull it out of the oven because the residual heat in the cast iron will cook it the rest of the way (keep the thermometer in there to know when it's done). It's ok if the inside is a little pink when you slice it, so long as the thickest part reads 145.
  2. Could hardly taste the dill on the pork, could probably omit or use some other herb, maybe like dried rosemary.
  3. Used couscous instead of farro, and roasted what veggies I had on hand: sweet potatoes, red onion, and summer squash separately from the pork and tomatoes. Just quickly look up how long to roast each type of veggie (you could sub others) and group by pan accordingly.
  4. Definitely do the lemon vinaigrette for the seasoning, and add more lemon if needed after you toss it all together.
  5. Omitted the feta.
