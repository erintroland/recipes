Sweet Potato and Spinach Chilaquiles

April 27, 2015

This one I made up with stuff in the fridge, and it was delicious! I had some leftover corn chips, one sweet potato, half a bag of spinach, some cilantro, and some salsa. I cooked the sweet potato in the microwave, the chopped it up and seared it a bit in some olive oil. Then I added the chips, let them fry up, then added the spinach in at the end to wilt. I topped with salsa, a fried egg, and cilantro. This fed me for three meals and it was very fast. Will definitely make again!
