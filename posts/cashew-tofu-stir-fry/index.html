Cashew Tofu Stir Fry

March 24, 2021

Make this in batches and you can have a larger stir fry meal. Try putting a little more veggis etc in the wok and see how it goes.

Make sure enough oil to fry the tofu in so it's crispy. Drain on paper towels while you make the rest of the dish. Add tofu on top of the serving dish or bowl so it stays crispy.

Only use 2tbsp honey instead of 3.
