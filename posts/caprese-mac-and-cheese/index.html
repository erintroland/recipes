Caprese Mac and Cheese

November 5, 2020

A great twist on classic mac and cheese. Loved the cherry tomatoes which provided a burst of juicy flavor.

Used regular mustard and threw in some arugula I had on hand. Also, was a bit undersalted even with all the cheese. I used salted butter since that's what I had(more salty), and subbed about half of the parmesan with mozzarella (less salty), but omitted the added salt (less salty), so maybe needed a bit of salt still. Make sure to taste the sauce as you go! It was undersalted again following the recipe this time (and not as good without shredded mozzarella, used the TJ's Quattro Formaggio blend). But would definitely make again.
