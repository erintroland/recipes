Bean Soup

October 15, 2023

This was delicious.

Double the recipe and use dried beans. You don't need to cook them separately, just saute the aromatics and tomato paste, then add in the dried beans and liquid, It took take about 45 mins to cook with navy beans I soaked for 24h.

Add in some baby spinach to add a vegetable to the dish.

I didn't like the picada all that much-- make the arugula pesto and top with that.
