Homemade Salsa (Canned Tomatoes)

August 24, 2021

Very quick and easy!

Used the immersion blender and some of the jarred chili paste for heat instead of the jalapeno. You could really use any hot sauce in it to taste. Also used two small cans of canned diced tomatoes instead of a large can of whole ones.

Blended the onion, garlic, and cilantro with the lime juice inside one of the tomato cans because it was the right size., then incorporated with the tomatoes.
