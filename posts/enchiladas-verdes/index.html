Enchiladas Rojas

October 17, 2015

These are pretty versatile and yummy!

1. Fillings can be meat, beans, veggies, cheese, or some combo of each. For more crunchy veggies like onion/bell pepper, or spinach, you really need to saute them first. They won't cook enough in the oven and the texture is off.

2. The red sauce linked below was amazing, I made it with smoked paprika instead of chili powder and added some red pepper flakes. Sauce recipe is good with just chili powder too. Cut the salt a bit, and don't over-sauce the enchiladas. Yes it is possible.

3. Make sure you have nearly a full block of Aldi cheese for these. And scatter any leftover filling on top with the shredded cheese!
