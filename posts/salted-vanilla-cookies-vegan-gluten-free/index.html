Salted Vanilla Cookies (Vegan Gluten Free)

November 28, 2025

These were not bad given they don't have the usual ingredients.

  1. They are extremely crumbly coming off the baking sheet. Take a lot of care getting them off. But they firmed up pretty nicely after they cooled.
  2. Go heavy on the flaky salt at the end. It's good, but it tends to fall off so more is better.
  3. Used light brown sugar as a 1:1 substitute for coconut sugar.
  4. Could try vegan chocolate chips in the recipe?
