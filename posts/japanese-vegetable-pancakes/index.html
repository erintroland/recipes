Japanese Vegetable Pancakes

November 17, 2020

These were easy and delicious--about an hour start to finish with cleanup.

I used some "asian blend" greens from the farmer's market instead of kale.

Topped it with some sauce (mayo, soy sauce, a bit of honey), pickled onion, sesame seeds. Really tasted like the real thing to me!

Also it looks like the mixture has too many veggies and not enough batter, but they fried up just fine. My batch made 5 1-cup pancakes, and I cooked them in a medium nonstick one at a time. Reheated pretty well too.
