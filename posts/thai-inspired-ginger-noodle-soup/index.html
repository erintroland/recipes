Thai Inspired Ginger Noodle Soup

October 15, 2023

Wow! I riffed on this while recovering from a stomach bug and it was fantastic.

I used tofu, kale, and mushrooms. No curry paste, just a bit of harissa.

I used onions instead of shallot.

You can just dump the rice noodles into the broth, you don't have to cook them separately. And skip the turmeric, it doesn't add much and just makes this stain easier.
