Lebanese Moussaka (Gluten Free/Vegan)

March 29, 2026

→ View recipe

This was so delicious! And pretty easy to make. Makes around 4 portions with quinoa. Notes:
1. Roast the eggplant for like an hour, even 40 mins wasn't quite enough.And put a lot of oil on it.
2. Add more salt--this kind of got less salty over time as the veggies released their water. Note that the recipe doesn't tell you when to add salt, so just do when when you know you're supposed to (salt the eggplant, the sauteed veggies, etc).
3. I served with quinoa, when you cook it, add some salt and/or oil bc by itself it just diluted everything and made it taste more bland.
4. Don't skip the lemon zest as garnish! And can put some good olive oil too.
5. Added some red pepper flakes for heat.
6. Drizzle some good olive oil on top to serve.
