Thai Peanut and Quinoa Salad

June 9, 2021

Delicious and versatile! Would definitely make again. 3/4 cup quinoa cooks up to a pretty decent amount--got 4 meals out of this! A great grain salad with lots of substitution options for the veggies.

Update: Used the Bright Cabbage Slaw recipe in Salt, Acid, Fat, Heat and did the Mexican variation. Added quinoa and white beans--wow so delicious/flavorful and better than this original recipe!! Would add another can of white beans next time. There is also an Asian inspired version, so try that with quinoa as well!
