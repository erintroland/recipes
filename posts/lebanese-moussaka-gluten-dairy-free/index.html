Lebanese Moussaka (gluten/dairy free)

March 23, 2026

So delicious!

  1. Put a lot of oil on the eggplant and roast for around 40 mins. 20-25 was not long enough.
  2. Added some red pepper flakes for heat.
  3. Eat with quinoa to replace couscous.
  4. Salt as you go, the recipe doesn't include any salting instructions.
  5. Finish with some good olive oil and lemon zest!
  6. This makes around 4 portions if served with quinoa.
