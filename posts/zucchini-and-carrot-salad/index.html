Zucchini and Carrot Salad

April 12, 2015

Idea from Cal Peternell's Twelve Recipes. Use a vegetable peeler to peel zucchini and carrots into thin, fat strips. Add some fresh herbs, oil, lemon juice salt and pepper. Yum!
