Savory Bread Pudding

September 27, 2021

Ok, going to keep tweaking this one until I get it right!

  1. 6 cups of your bread is a little denser than the bread in this recipe. So add more liquid (probably just more milk) until you cover the bread cubes.
  2. More more more fixins--I had to sub in a medium onion for the leeks and scallions for the chives. I would double both of them. And add in more arugula (not in recipe but good). This may also help it be less dry. And add more dried thyme.
  3. Could also easily add in mushrooms, summer squash. Maybe broccoli.
  4. It's pretty easy to assemble but you have to let it rest 15 mins and then bake for an hour, so plan ahead.
