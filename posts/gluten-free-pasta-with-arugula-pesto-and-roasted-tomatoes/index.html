Gluten free pasta with arugula pesto and roasted tomatoes

July 11, 2026

→ View recipe

Very easy and great for summer! Roast some tomatoes in the air fryer (if cherry, buy at least two pints). Toss with the pesto and some marinated artichokes and vegan mozzarella if you have it.

Start with juice of half a lemon, then add more if needed. Came out too acidic most recent time.
