Black Bean Coconut Soup

January 23, 2022

Added some diced carrots and sweet potatoes and simmered the soup until those were done. Threw in a little more tomato paste since the volume of stuff in the soup was bigger than the original recipe


No fresh mango or even their substitute of cilantro-so that's why I added the pickled onions and sour cream on top.


I also used a few tbsp of chipotles in adobo sauce I had in the freezer as the spicy component, but you could use any hot sauce or spice to taste.
