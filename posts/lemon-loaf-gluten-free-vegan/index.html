Lemon Loaf (Gluten Free/Vegan)

March 29, 2026

This turned out so nice! A few notes:

  1. Used tapioca starch instead of potato starch, since that's what I had on hand. Following directions from this site, I added an additional 1/4 cup tapioca starch and subtracted off 1/4 cup almond flour.
  2. Note that you need lemon zest and juice for the loaf and the glaze. I zested 4 lemons and that was enough, but make sure to set aside some for the glaze, and don't toss the extra lemons until you do the glaze.
  3. I put this in the oven for 55 minutes. Even then, it was a little soft. So in my oven, this took a lot longer than the recipe timing of 35-40 mins.
  4. Defrosting some frozen berries and adding a splash of orange blossom water is a delicious accompaniment after doing the glaze.
