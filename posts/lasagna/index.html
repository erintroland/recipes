Lasagna!

March 28, 2019

Yum! Despite making a substitution for nearly single ingredient in this recipe, it turned out fantastic.

1. I used a half cup sour cream half cup heavy cream in place of creme fraiche. I used crushed tomatoes and diced tomatoes instead of whole.

2. Per smitten kitchen, you can just soak the noodles in piping hot tap water for 10 mins and there is no need to actually cook them ahead of time or buy the no boil, par cooked noodles. Just cover the lasagna in foil brushed with oil for the first half of baking.

3. Fill the pan high so you can use all the noodles, then put a baking sheet lined with foil beneath to catch the spills.

4. Next time, add more cheese and filling! A small box of mushrooms, a small box of spinach, and a medium onion wasn't nearly enough. You may have to do the filling in batches in the big metal skillet to get enough. Smitten kitchen recommends 5 oz baby spinach, a large onion, then 4 additional cups of other veggies (mushrooms, bell pepper, zuchinni, eggplant, etc).
