Spinach Stuffed Shells

March 24, 2021

Great first run, would try again to see if these can be really good.

To do again:

  1. Use frozen spinach, squeeze it out tightly over a strainer. Then use a big bowl and the immersion blender to make the filling.
  2. Use canned plum tomatoes, crushed, as the sauce. No need to make or buy another sauce.

For next time:

  1. Make sure filling is salted enough, and if it tastes really garlicky raw just go with it and see what happens.
  2. Try to find full fat ricotta.
  3. Could make more than 28 shells, had leftover filling.
