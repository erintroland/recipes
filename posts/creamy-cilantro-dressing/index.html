Creamy Cilantro Dressing

November 30, 2020

This is easy and versatile! Lots of possibilities with the herbs and dairy. I used sour cream instead of yogurt and half the salt called for. It was almost too salty with that much, so maybe even less next time. I put it on some cabbage I had on hand and a baked potato. You could put it on a sandwich, roasted veggies, just about anything really.

Update: Using full fat yogurt, it came out perfect so long as you follow the recipe with the following modifications: don't add any additional water, don't add the sugar (but keep the honey), and reduce the salt to a half teaspoon and some change.
