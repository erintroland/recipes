Pioneer Woman's Chili

January 23, 2016

This was so good! And so very easy, so long as you've got an hour to let it simmer. The only few things I'd change for my personal tastes:

1. Break up the ground meat more so it's in much smaller chunks. 2. Use 1 lb instead of two lb ground meat. 3. Use tomato paste plus water instead of tomato sauce (which has sugar unless homemade). 4. Use one more can of beans. 5. Add in diced bell pepper and carrot to throw in some veggies. But don't skimp on cutting the veggies small enough--big chunks aren't good. If adding veggies, give the meat some time to cook in the spices and tomato paste before adding in the veggies and beans. Three large carrots and two bell peppers are enough.

6. Adding a large diced sweet potato instead of carrot is delicious! And makes it a more complete meal.

6. Adding in some beer (dark if available) before you let it simmer adds a nice flavor.
