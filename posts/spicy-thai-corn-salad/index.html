Spicy Thai Corn Salad

July 17, 2022

lkajdlkjad

So good! Takes a little while with all the chopping/veggie prep but it's worth it. Makes enough for 6 meals if you make rice with it. It's pretty versatile, you could add bell pepper, shredded summer squash, cucumber.

  1. Use > 1tbsp sambal olek, it wasn't spicy enough
  2. Chop the tomatoes into smaller pieces, the larger slices weren't doing it for me.
  3. I wonder if you could put this in a ceramic bowl and grind it up with a mug or something instead of using a zip lock bag every time.
  4. Used a whole container of green beans from the farmer's market instead of just 3/4 cup/
