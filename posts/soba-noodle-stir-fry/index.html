Soba Noodle Stir Fry

March 19, 2022

Wow, this turned out amazing! Combined a version of Smitten Kitchen's recipe with The Kitchn's stir fry technique! This could work with a variety of veggies.

  1. Had arugula, portabello mushrooms, and peanuts on hand and they worked beautifully.
  2. Used 4 soba noodles bundles.
  3. Never put the noodles into the wok-they tend to stick.
  4. As always used Sambal Olek for heat.
