Black Pepper Tofu and Eggplant

September 24, 2019

Wow, this was so amazingly good! It took a long time to make but I think for a second go around it could go a lot faster. It is a fair amount of chopping and uses a lot of bowls and pans.

1. Two medium eggplants were a good amount of eggplant. 2. Use a square baking pan for the tofu and the large baking sheet for the eggplant. The eggplant cooked faster. 3. I added some cashews I had lying around to the sauce at the end and some fresh basil on top once the whole dish came together. These two additions were incredibly delicious. Sesame seeds would probably also be good. 4. Target had the extra firm tofu in with the vegetables near the carrots and bagged salad. 5. Tried with cauliflower and sweet potato. Sweet potato was definitely better. I'd say anything that is slightly sweet when roasted and can absorb the sauce would work well. So eggplant, squash, sweet potato. 6. No need to heat the pans in the oven with oil on them. I had them burn once when I tried this, so I tried without and it came out fine. 7. Next time: Try lightly greasing the tofu pan if you don't have a thin spatula to remove the tofu when they're done.
