Eggplant Caponata with Couscous and Goat Cheese

September 20, 2021

Wow! This was a really great late summer/early fall meal. A few things to note:

  1. Two long, thin, med/large Japanese eggplants seem like a lot chopped up, but they cook down. I think you could have them somewhat close together and overlapping to fit them all on the half sheet pan. Try putting it all on there and see what happens. Another option is to put excess eggplant on the large cookie sheet with no sides with olive oil and salt and pepper, then combine with the main pan with the stuff that does fit (including some of the eggplant + the rest of the ingredients) at the end. Ended up doing separately roasting some of the eggplant anyways this time and it turned out fine.
  2. You have to use a pan with sides!
  3. Everything was cooked after 20 mins--this may be because I had a relatively small volume of veggies on the pan.
  4. If you're going to add the pepitas/pine nuts to the second round of roasting, don't pre-roast them! Seems like they'd just burn!
  5. EASY on the vinegar. You can always add more.
  6. Try toasting the grains first if you feel ambitious--I used quinoa bc that's what I had on hand.
