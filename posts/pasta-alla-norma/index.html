Pasta alla Norma

November 6, 2018

This was labor intensive but delicious.

1. I used 2 28 oz cans + 1 14 oz can of tomatoes and basically just simmered them with the onions for as long as the rest of the recipe took. Then I used the immersion blender. Next time I'd cook the onions a bit first to get them soft, then add in the tomatoes.

2. Waaaay too much salt for the eggplant. Cut it down some. Dice it by cutting off the bottom to form a flat base, then cutting down long, thin slices whih can then be cubed.

3. I used three medium eggplants, which is way less than the recipe calls for. It was plenty for me. It took 5 batches to fry all of it.

4. Take the time to peel the eggplant. Eggplant skin is kind of weird and tough.

5. Lots of pots. Had to use the cast iron pot for the pasta.

6. Substitued queso fresco for ricotta salata.

7. Used 1.5 boxes of penne. It was plenty.
