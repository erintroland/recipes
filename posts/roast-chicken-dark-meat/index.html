Roast Chicken (Dark Meat)

March 29, 2026

Pat chicken dry

Season with oil salt and pepper

Don’t crowd sheet pan

Preheat 425 give enough time to heat

After put in, reduce to 375

Flip it after 5 mins

Wait 10-15 mins, then cut one open and check it. Will probably need more time. You undercooked it the first time you tried this.

Make sure juices are clear. Meat might have little streaks of pink.

Can overcook a little if in doubt bc it’s dark meat

You used skinless boneless drumstick meat this first time. Thighs might be different.
