Indian Spiced Fried Rice

November 10, 2020

Delicious! I omitted the curry leaves and chili paste instead of fresh chiles, and cashews instead of peanuts.

  1. Pan Am Market sells black sesame seeds.
  2. This isn't really fried rice, you don't actually fry the rice. You used leftover cooked rice and put other stuff in it.
  3. Can cook in wok or large frying pan.
  4. Can add in some veggies like chopped asparagus or green beans or arugula (easiest is to just saute or use below method with a frying pan.
  5. Up the rice and nuts to get more servings.
  6. I like it with cashews better instead of peanuts.
  7. Double the onions.
  8. Add shrimp! Defrost a few mins in some water, then toss with salt (not too much), pepper, and coriander, then sautee for a couple of mins on each side.

Added in some green beans cooked a la Salt Acid Fat Heat and it was delicious! On the beans, don't take the lid off that often, make just move the beans around with the lid on by moving the pan, bc the water came out quickly.
