Bob’s Red Mill Fluffy Gluten Free Pancake Recipe

July 11, 2026

→ View recipe

Yum! And so easy! In the time it takes to clean up after making the batter, it has sat long enough to be ready to cook.

I cut the sugar in half, try next time with same amount of salt as recipe calls for (I like a slightly saltier taste).

Works great with flax egg (1 egg = 1 tbsp water 3 tbsp ground flax, stir and let set for a few mins).

Have the induction burner at 1000W, then adjust down as needed as you make multiple batches.

It makes around 8-9 pancakes with 1/4 c batter. Scant 1/4 cup makes 9, fuller 1/4 cup makes 8.
