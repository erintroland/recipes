Hearty Sweet Potato, Arugula, and Wild Rice Salad

March 19, 2023

This was really good! A great template to use up veggies you have on hand. A few things for next time:

  1. Just use regular vinaigrette. The one in the recipe isn't worth the complication.
  2. Up the rice to arugula ratio a bit, and roast two cans more chickpeas to make this heartier. Or, could wilt the arugula a bit to make the salad more dense. It wasn't quite enough packed in a tupperware to fill me up for a lunch.
  3. Don't overdo it on the sweet potatoes, otherwise the dish becomes overly sweet
  4. Pomegranate seeds were delicious in this!
