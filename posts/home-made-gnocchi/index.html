Home! Made! Gnocchi!

May 16, 2021

Wow, so good and so easy! Never heard of ricotta gnocchi before and it is so much easier to make than potato gnocchi. Could use with tons of different sauces etc.

Tips:

  1. Don't forget to put pot on to boil.
  2. I made an easy tomato sauce with some canned whole tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil. Then I sauteed some veggies in a separate pan, then combined everything together. The recipe's sauce is good, but it's easier to make this one. And I was done in an hour. You could make this with pesto or cream sauce, so many possibilities!
  3. I don't understand how one can "slide" gnocchi from a baking pan into the boiling water all at once. I used a slotted spoon to get them into the water a few at a time.
  4. Timing: with prepping the leeks, probably takes 1h15 - 1h30 to make.
  5. Add only 1 tbsp of lemon juice! You added 1/4c. And don't overdo it on the zest in the gnocchi, either.
  6. Don't overcook the peas. Thaw them a bit before using, but don't cook them before throwing them in the sauce, they will cook there.
  7. You could use all kinds of other sauces and veggies with it.
