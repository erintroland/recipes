Curry Chicken Salad

March 29, 2026

→ View recipe

Yum! And very easy and versatile. Serve with gluten free wraps.

1. 1lb of boneless skinless dark meat made a lot, probably like 5 meals.
2. Roast chicken according to separate roast chicken recipe post.
3. Use vegan mayo.
4. I added in some sliced apple and some random veggies I had on hand (spinach, bell pepper)
You roughly want the 3x egg salad volume to create enough curried mayo sauce for 1 lb of chicken.
