Chana Masala

March 25, 2018

Delicious! And very easy. Great leftovers, would definitely make again. Makes about 5 servings with rice. It's pretty quick, the longest part is chopping the onion, garlic, ginger, etc. Would be a little much for a weeknight but a nice easy recipe for a weekend.

1. I added in a red bell pepper to boost veggies. 2. I didn't have turmeric or chili pepper, but I added Sambal Olek chili sauce instead. Like a scant half teaspoon was enough. 3. I added the salt when I was sauteing the onions, etc instead of at the end. 4. I used lemon juice instead of amchoor powder per the recipe note. Juice from half of one large lemon was plenty to give it a nice sour note. 5. Next time, DEFINITELY try using a large 28oz can of crushed tomatoes to make it saucier.
