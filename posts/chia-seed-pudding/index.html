Chia Seed Pudding

August 4, 2015

Delicious! Great texture after sitting overnight. The chia seeds expand and become like little tapioca balls. I wouldn't add the vanilla flavoring though, it tastes a little fake. Next time, I'd just keep it simple: no sweetener, no vanilla, unflavored milk, then add fruit.
