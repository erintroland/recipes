Homemade Currant Jam

July 14, 2021

Yum yum! And fairly easy! Only two ingredients because currants have natural pectin.

NOTE: The linked recipe is for much more than you usually buy (recipe is for ~4 small containers of currants from the farmer's market). You can weigh what you have and scale appropriately (2 of those containers is ~2 cups which is ~1/2 quart).

  1. Be careful not to overcook--it doesn't need long once it's boiling. You can take it off when it's fairly runny, it will firm up a lot in the fridge. Have way overcooked it in the past. If it's too runny you can always reheat it.
  2. Put the currants in the large rectangle tupperware and crush with the large metal serving spoon.
  3. It doesn't take long for the sugar to dissolve (think simple syrup). What looks like sugar after a few mins is probably bubbles or small bits of fruit.
