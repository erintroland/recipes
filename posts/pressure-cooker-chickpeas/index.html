Pressure Cooker Chickpeas

October 22, 2018

Yum!! These were so delicious. I had them with rice, wilted spinach, ricotta, and homemade breadcrumbs, but Smitten Kitchen lists so many other possibilities!

Make sure to soak for at least a few hours ahead of time, and add plenty of water to the pot. The chickpeas absorb a lot of water and you'll want the broth.

Great with some rice, good olive oil, and veggies like shredded carrots or zucchini. Wasn't great with fresh greens, wilted would be better. Probably good with roasted veggies too.

ALERT: Goya brand chickpeas took an hour to cook (not including the time to come to pressure). Don't use them.
